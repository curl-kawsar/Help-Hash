Our Donation Plan

We are currently planning to help people by providing food and medicine. A majority of the refugees suffer from food scarcity, and there's also a significant portion dealing with illnesses. To address these issues, we intend to distribute food and medicine, aiming to assist at least 1000 people. Additionally, we plan to help by donating blood. Once the disaster subsides, we aim to distribute educational materials like pens, paper, and books.

Food For A Family

In critical disaster times, people suffer a lot due to food scarcity. We plan to distribute essential dry food items to address this issue.

Blood For People

Blood is a crucial necessity in emergency situations. We aim to offer support by providing blood to those in need.

Medicine For People

For individuals suffering from various illnesses, we aim to provide necessary medicines to alleviate their conditions.
